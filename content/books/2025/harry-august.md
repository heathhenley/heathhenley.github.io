By Claire North

Finished: 11/23/25

In this universe, there are certain types of people who are reborn when they die - they're given the chance to relive their life over again with knowledge of the previous live(s). Interesting concept, had a bit of a slow moving middle and then picked up. Overall I enjoyed it.
